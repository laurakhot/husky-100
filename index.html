Welcome to My Husky 100 Portfolio

My Husky experience has been defined by exploration and by connecting the dots between my diverse experiences from switching majors and engaging with new communities to taking on different jobs and tackling challenges across various disciplines. That curiosity became the way I learn: questioning, experimenting, and finding meaning in the intersections of my experiences.

That spirit of exploration ultimately led me to Informatics, where I found language for what I had been practicing all along: connecting technical knowledge with human-centered impact. At UW, I have come to see technology not just as a academic discipline, but as a powerful tool for education, equity, and empowerment both inside and beyond the classroom. This perspective was shaped most deeply through my work as a teaching assistant for CSE courses and my involvement in the Web Impact RSO, where I saw that learning does not stop at understanding a concept, it deepens when that knowledge is shared, applied, and adapted to real people and real needs.

My UW Journey

Fall 2022

Admitted as ENGRUD

Started my journey at UW as an Engineering Undeclared student, initially intending to study bioengineering while exploring different engineering paths.

Winter 2023

Took CSE 121

What started as a prerequisite quickly became a turning point in my journey. Despite initial struggles with this entirely new subject, I discovered a passion for computing and problem-solving through code.

Summer 2023

Declared ECE Major

Officially placed into the Electrical and Computer Engineering major, diving deeper into hardware and software systems.

Fall 2023

Joined Web Impact

Began attending Web Impact workshops on a friend’s suggestion, hoping to learn new web development skills and apply them to real-world, community-driven projects.

Fall 2024

Started TAing

Began my role as a Teaching Assistant, supporting students in learning software development while deepening my own knowledge and passion for education and mentorship.

Fall 2024

Became Web Impact Coding Officer

As a Coding Officer, I mentored technical teams and led the workshop series, bridging my teaching experience with my skills in web development.

Winter 2025

Switched to Informatics

Made the decision to pursue Informatics to focus my studies on human-centered technology, accessibility, and creating solutions with real-world impact.

Fall 2025

Became Web Impact President

As President, I lead student teams to deliver digital solutions for local businesses and community organizations, shaping the club’s vision in a role that brings together my teaching, technical, and leadership experiences.
