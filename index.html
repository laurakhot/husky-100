Welcome to My Husky 100 Portfolio

My Husky experience has been defined by exploration and by connecting the dots between my diverse experiences from switching majors and engaging with new communities to taking on different jobs and tackling challenges across various disciplines. That curiosity became the way I learn: questioning, experimenting, and finding meaning in the intersections of my experiences.

That spirit of exploration ultimately led me to Informatics, where I found language for what I had been practicing all along: connecting technical knowledge with human-centered impact. At UW, I have come to see technology not just as a academic discipline, but as a powerful tool for education, equity, and empowerment both inside and beyond the classroom. This perspective was shaped most deeply through my work as a teaching assistant for CSE courses and my involvement in the Web Impact RSO, where I saw that learning does not stop at understanding a concept, it deepens when that knowledge is shared, applied, and adapted to real people and real needs.
