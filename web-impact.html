Leading Web Impact Forward

As President, I've focused on scaling our impact while supporting high-quality, long-lasting deliverables. I introduced dedicated Project Manager roles to bring Agile workflows into our structure, making our projects more professional and our teams more effective.

I've also spearheaded our web development workshop series, designing it to mirror the end-to-end process from client discovery to deployment. Additionally, I created a workshop website that documents our curriculum, providing a resource that will continue teaching and guiding students even after I graduate. Through these efforts, I've aimed to leave structures in place that amplify both learning and impact.
